Passwords in the Wild

In today’s digital age, protecting your online identity and personal information has become more crucial than ever. Why Password Hygiene Matters Every day, cybercriminals attempt to exploit weak passwords to gain unauthorized access to sensitive personal, financial, and professional information. According to Verizon’s 2023 Data Breach Investigations Report, compromised passwords account for 81% of hacking-related breaches.
